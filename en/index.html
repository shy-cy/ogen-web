What is Ogen Center?

An Anchor of Belonging in Cyprus

Life abroad opens the door to new opportunities, but also presents a real challenge: how do you preserve the language, culture, and connection to Israel? Ogen Center was created to answer that question.

We're building an open, welcoming community home where Israelis and Jews of all ages can learn, meet, celebrate, and feel part of a vibrant, living community.

Our purpose

Why Ogen?

Thousands of Israelis and Jews have made Cyprus their home. Children are growing up in international settings, Hebrew is heard less in daily life, and the connection to Israeli culture and Jewish heritage can fade over the years. Ogen Center was born from the understanding that a strong community needs a home, a steady place to meet, learn, celebrate, create, and belong.

Our Vision

To be the cultural, educational, and community home for Israelis and Jews in Cyprus. A place where Hebrew is heard, Israeli culture lives and breathes, and the community grows together, generation after generation, as connections are formed.
